About Us

We, B.M.C.R.C. (Dr. B. C. Roy Memorial Complementary Medicines Research Centre) are registered under Act XXVI of 1961 by the Govt. of West Bengal & under Scientific Institution Act 1854. Our Registration No. is S/76111. Our establisment is promoting of Complementary Medicine. B.M.C.R.C. is Affiliated with THE OPEN INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITY FOR COMPLEMENTARY MEDICINES, (Medicina Alternativa), which is established as per World Health Organisation, Alma Ata, U.S.S.R. Declaration 1962. The University is recognised by the United Nations Peace University costituted under resoulation No. 35/55/5/XII/80 of August 15th 1988.

Admission Process

1. Admission is open to all eligible candidates of India and from abroad also, both male and female with no age bar.

2. A candidate seeking admission will have to fill up the prescribed application form in his / her own handwriting properly enclosed with all necessary documents.

3. The selection committees of the B.M.C.R.C. reserves the rights of scrutiny and selection of the candidate.

4. Any dispute what soever shall be settled in civil court of Kolkata.
